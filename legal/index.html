Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal data in Skynet is very important to us and we're always committed to improve encryption and reduce the amount of data collected.

Encryption

The connection between the client and the server is generally protected through transport encryption. This makes sure that the data cannot be accessed by third parties. In addition, certain data is end-to-end encrypted so that even we as the server owner cannot access it. However, providing our services requires us to have access to specific data.

Data which can be read by us include:

  • Your Skynet contacts
  • Your 'last seen' state
  • When and to whom you send a message
  • Members and admins of a group
  • Your email and nickname
  • Your status contents
  • Your profile image
  • Your daystream

Data which cannot be read by anybody include:

  • The contents of your messages
  • The contents of files you send in private or group chats
  • The group title and the group image
  • The keys of your conversations

Data processing

Part of the data listed above must be stored to allow us to synchronize all of your devices. We guarantee that we will not share this data with anyone and that we will not use it for any other purpose than to provide the features of Skynet. However, we cannot guarantee that this data is not disclosed due to hacker attacks or on request by a public authority.
